GAJAR HALWA

Description

Gajar ka halwa, also known as gajorer halua, gajrela, gajar pak, and carrot halwa is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding made by placing grated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water, milk and sugar, cardamom and then cooking while stirring regularly.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • 1 ½ quarts whole milk
  • 2 pounds carrots, grated
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • ½ cup raisins or sultanas
  • 1 ½ teaspoons ground cardamom
  • 1 pinch saffron threads
  • 8 tablespoons chopped pistachio nuts

Steps

  1. Heat ghee in a large saucepan over medium heat and add carrots.
  2. Cook and stir briefly, about 3 minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and increase heat; bring to a boil and cook, stirring constantly, for 5 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer gently, uncovered, and stirring occasionally until moisture evaporates, about 1 hour.
  5. Bring carrot mixture back to a boil; add sugar, raisins, cardamom, and saffron.
  6. Cook and stir until mixture has thickened, about 10 minutes.
  7. Ladle carrot halwa into 8 individual serving dishes.
  8. Top each serving with 1 tablespoon pistachios.
  9. Serve warm or chill before serving.
